About Me
General Info
I've been interested in Technology for most of my life, and since the creation of the Android and iOS platforms, I've wanted to become a Programmer. Currently, I have been programming for three years and I have experience with Java, HTML, CSS, Dart, XML, JavaScript, and C#, as well as the Android platform. My experience with technology, as well as the introduction of Material Design from Google has also given me an interest in icon and UI design, which I am currently learning now. As well as icon/UI design, games, anime, and minimalist art have influenced my style in design.
  
My goal
I want to influence the world in a positive way with my code. One way I want to do this is to help those that suffer from mental issues like depression or anxiety disorders, to reduce the risk of suicide and self harm in them, and to hopefully eliminate it all together. Another goal is to influence how code and technology is handeled, and to make things simpler for both developers and consumers.
